# School Connect Smart Pages Product Overview

## Introduction

School Connect Smart Pages helps schools turn ordinary documents into clean, editable, reviewable digital pages.

It is designed for school administration work such as notices, circulars, timetables, programmes, meeting minutes, exam schedules, policies, and other general school documents.

Smart Pages is a separate product area from Report Lab. It is not the marksheet import flow and should not be described as one.

## What Smart Pages Does

Smart Pages can:

- Create new school documents in an editable workspace.
- Upload a document for extraction and review.
- Turn parsed content into a polished page using templates.
- Let staff edit the extracted text before generating the final output.
- Keep version history for generated documents.
- Publish finished documents to a secure public link.
- Download finished documents as PDF and use print preview.
- Organize work with collections and bulk generation.
- Show billing and credit usage for Smart Pages work.

## Problems It Solves

- Reduces manual typing from scanned or photographed documents.
- Helps staff clean up untidy notes into formal school documents.
- Makes it easier to reuse one structure across many records.
- Gives schools a review step before they print or publish.
- Helps teams keep a clear record of versions, outputs, and published links.

## Document Upload and Intelligence Overview

Smart Pages supports uploaded files for school documents.

- PDF and image uploads are used for document extraction.
- CSV, XLS, and XLSX files are parsed locally as structured data.
- Word document uploads are not supported yet and are treated as a future enhancement.
- The extracted content is meant to be reviewed by a human before printing or publishing.

## Human Review Rule

Smart Pages output is a draft, not a final authority.

Staff should always review extracted text, tables, and generated output before printing, sharing, or relying on it.

## Data Accuracy Responsibility

School staff remain responsible for checking names, dates, amounts, marks, and any other sensitive information.

If a field is unclear, the correct action is to review it manually, not guess.

## Smart Pages vs Report Lab

Smart Pages:

- Works with school documents.
- Focuses on notices, circulars, schedules, minutes, and general school records.
- Uses document intelligence and editable templates.

Report Lab:

- Works with report generation and marksheet/report workflows.
- Is a separate module.
- Should not be used as the description for Smart Pages.
