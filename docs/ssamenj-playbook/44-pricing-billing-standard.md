# Pricing And Billing Standard

Pricing and billing must be centralized, explicit, and auditable.

## Rules

- Centralize prices.
- No scattered hardcoded prices.
- Offers have start, end, and status.
- Currency must be explicit.
- Do not mix UGX and AED in the same amount field.
- Invoices and payments are audited.
- Discounts require permission.
- Billing changes require audit.

## Required Fields Where Applicable

- Amount.
- Currency.
- Tax/fee breakdown.
- Offer or discount ID.
- Effective dates.
- Actor ID.
- Tenant/company/school/property scope.
- Audit event ID.
